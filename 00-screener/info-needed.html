We’ll ask for info about:

  • Your identiy
  • Your household (who you live and pay taxes with)
  • Your household income and expenses
  • Social security numbers for anyone you're applying for
  • Other benefits you use
  • Citizenship (your answer will not affect you or your family’s immigration status)

This information is kept confidential and secure as required by law

What does “household” mean?

In order to find out which programs you qualify for, we need to know about your household.

This is a list of who counts as part of your “household”:

  • Anyone you live with and share food/cook with, such as relatives and roommates.
  • Anyone who you file federal taxes with.
  • You spouse, if you are a married and live with them.
  • Your parents, if you are under 22 and live with them.
  • Your kids, if they are under 22 and live with you.
  • The other parent of your kids, if you live with them (even if you are separated or not married)

We'll take you through step by step. All of this info helps us get you benefits more quickly.
