  • How will this help me?

    To qualify for 3 Day Expedited Service, your income is:

    • less than $150 in monthly income and have less than $100 on hand right now
    • or your household’s combined monthly income and money on hand are less than your household’s combined monthly rent and utilities
    • or you are a migrant or seasonal farm worker and have less $100 on hand right now
