What happens next?

SNAP

Before you can get your SNAP benefits, you'll have to be interviewed over the phone or at a local SNAP office.

You will receive a letter/email/text with instructions for setting up your interview.

Medicaid

In the 7 to 10 days, you will get a Medicaid card in the mail and your healthcare will begin. If you have any problems, call your local office at 555-555-5555
