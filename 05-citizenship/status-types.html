Read through this list. Do you fit any of these immigration categories?
  • Lawful Permanent Resident (LPR / Green Card Holder)
  • Asylee
  • Refugee
  • Cuban / Haitian entrant
  • Paroled into the U.S. for at least one year
  • Conditional entrant granted before 1980
  • Non-citizen spouse, child, or parent survivor of domestic violence.
  • Victim of trafficking
  • A trafficking victim’s spouse, child, sibling, or parent
  • Have a pending application for a victim of trafficking visa
  • Granted witholding of deportation
  • Member of a federally recognized Indian tribe or American Indian born in Canada
Why do we ask this?

Immigrants in these categories may qualify for some programs. We ask about your immigration status so that we can find out what you may qualify for.

Applying signing up for these programs will not affect your or your family’s immigration status.

This information is private and kept confidential. It will not be shared with any agency for any reason other than checking your eligibility.
