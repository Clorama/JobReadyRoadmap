Read through this list. Do you fit any of these immigrant categories?
  • Lawful Permanent Resident (LPR / Green Card Holder)
  • Asylee
  • Refugee
  • Cuban / Haitian entrant
  • Paroled into the U.S. for at least one year
  • Conditional entrant granted before 1980
  • Battered non-citizen, spouses, child, or parent
  • Victim of trafficking
  • A trafficking victim’s spouse, child, sibling, or parent
  • Have a pending application for a victim of trafficking visa
  • Granted witholding of deportation
  • Member of a federally recognized Indian tribe or American Indian born in Canada
Why do we ask this?

Immigrants in these categores may qualify for some programs. We ask about your immigration status so that we can find out what you may qualify for.

Applying signing up for these programs will not affect you or your family’s immigration status.

This info is private and confidential.

To sign up for these services, we may need to ask for immigration document numbers later.
