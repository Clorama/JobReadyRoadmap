Why do we ask this?

In order to find out which programs you qualify for, we need to know about the family you live with and/or file federal taxes with.

This is a list of who we need to know about:

  • Anyone who you file federal taxes with.
  • Your spouse, if you are a married and live with them.
  • Your parents, if you are under 22 and live with them.
  • Your kids, if they are under 22 and live with you.
  • The other parent of your kids, if you live with them (even if you are separated or not married)

This is usually family members who you live with.
