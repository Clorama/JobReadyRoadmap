Welcome!

Everyone deserves a second chance and yet, the road to starting over can be stressful.

This site is here to help you get back on your feet! This site will make sure that journey back to re-employment will be as smooth and easy as possible. We are rooting for your success!

Programs that can help you:

Re-Entry Assistance

Partners for Re-Entry Center

Nonprofit that provides comprehensive reentry support including employment services, transitional housing assistance, counseling and mentoring.

Alaska Native Re-Entry Assistance

Alask Native Justice Center & CITC

Two nonprofits that provides comprehensive reentry support including employment services, transitional housing assistance, counseling and mentoring.

We will walk you through what you need to do for successful re-entry in 3 steps!

  • Check to see what benefits/services are available to you along your path.
  • Answer honestly a few questions about yourself and your needs for the best results.
  • If at any point you get lost or confused or need to stop, just hit the save button and you will be directed to a variety of ways to save your progress and pick up back where you left off whenever you are ready!
